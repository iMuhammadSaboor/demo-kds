Settings

Tune the KDS for the way your kitchen actually works — prep time targets, station assignments, how loud it barks at you.

Display behaviour

These settings apply to the kitchen-facing KDS screen.
Sound alerts on new orders
Short two-tone chime when a ticket hits the queue.
Flash late tickets
Orders past their prep target pulse red until bumped.
Auto-recall on accidental bump
If you bump twice in under 2 seconds, the second bump un-bumps instead. Saves mistakes during the rush.
Dense grid threshold
When queue exceeds this count, tiles shrink to fit more per screen.
